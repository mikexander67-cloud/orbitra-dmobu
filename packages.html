First-year bundle: add Care Plan with your launch package for Rs 28,000 — saves Rs 4,000 off the annual price. One-time, at point of launch. After year one, renews at standard tier pricing.

What's included (all tiers)

  • Hosting, domain, and business email kept active
  • Daily backups and security monitoring
  • Up to 2 minor content updates per month
  • Additional updates at Rs 1,500 each, billed transparently
  • Monthly performance snapshot

Commitment terms

  • Monthly: cancel any time with 30 days notice
  • Quarterly: cancel at end of quarter with 14 days notice
  • Semi-annual: cancel at end of term with 14 days notice
  • Annual: cancel at end of term with 30 days notice
  • You can upgrade to a longer commitment any time, with the price difference prorated

Prefer to self-manage? That's fine. Every package hands over full admin access, hosting and domain credentials in your name, a step-by-step manual, and recordings of training calls. Without an active Care Plan, Orbitra won't make updates on your behalf, monitor uptime, handle renewals, or restore your site if something breaks. One-off support sessions are available at Rs 1,500/hour, 30-minute minimum block — and you can subscribe to any Care Plan tier at any time, with no penalty for skipping it upfront.
